Step 1: Choose Your Models and Quantity

Apple iPhones come in multiple variants. Decide upfront:

  • Model: iPhone 16 Pro Max / Pro / Plus / standard, or previous generation
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB or 1TB (Pro/Max only)
  • Colour: Desert Titanium, Black Titanium, White Titanium, Natural Titanium
  • Quantity: wholesale typically starts from 5+ units, bulk from 20+ units

Step 3: Payment and Documentation

Standard B2B payment terms for iPhone wholesale from Dubai:

  • Wire transfer (T/T) is the most common method
  • Typically 30–50% deposit, remainder before shipment
  • Supplier provides commercial invoice, packing list, and certificate of origin
  • IMEI numbers listed on invoice for customs clearance

Step 4: Shipping and Customs

iPhones are high-value, low-weight goods — air freight is almost always used. Options from Dubai:

  • Express courier (DHL, FedEx, Aramex): 3–5 days, door-to-door, easiest for customs
  • Air cargo: cheaper per kg for large quantities, 2–4 days flight time

Import duty rates vary by country. Always confirm with your local customs authority before ordering. Most CIS countries have specific duty rates and value thresholds for smartphones.

Step 5: Verify Authenticity on Arrival

When your order arrives, verify each unit:

  • Check factory seal is intact
  • Verify IMEI on Apple's website (checkcoverage.apple.com)
  • Confirm model and storage match your invoice
  • Power on and complete initial setup to confirm unit is not activation locked
