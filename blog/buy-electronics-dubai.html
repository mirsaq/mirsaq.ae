Why Buy Electronics in Dubai vs. Your Home Country?

Dubai has significant pricing advantages for electronics buyers from most parts of the world:

  • No import duty on electronics entering Dubai — means UAE prices for iPhones and Samsung devices are among the lowest globally
  • All models available immediately — Dubai receives global device launches simultaneously. No waiting months for local release dates.
  • International (global) variants — devices sourced in Dubai are typically global variants that work on any network worldwide
  • Full manufacturer warranty — Apple, Samsung, Sony, Garmin and others honour their international warranties globally
  • Fast shipping — Dubai's air connections mean electronics reach Kazakhstan in 2–3 days, Nigeria in 3–5 days, UK in 2–4 days
