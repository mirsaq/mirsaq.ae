What "Genuine" Actually Means

When MIRSAQ says genuine, we mean:

  • Factory new — never activated, never repaired, never refurbished
  • Original packaging — with all box contents: charging cable, documentation, accessories as shipped by the manufacturer
  • Full manufacturer warranty — the standard international warranty from Apple, Samsung, Sony, Garmin, or whichever brand applies
  • Not grey market — sourced through official UAE distribution channels, not brought in through unofficial parallel import routes
  • Not carrier-locked (for smartphones) — unless specifically agreed, all devices are unlocked and work on any SIM worldwide

How You Can Verify When Your Order Arrives

When you receive your order from MIRSAQ, here is a simple checklist to independently verify authenticity:

Authenticity Verification Checklist

  • Check the factory seal is fully intact before opening
  • Verify the IMEI/serial number on the box matches the device (found in Settings on smartphones)
  • Check IMEI on Apple's checkcoverage.apple.com or Samsung's warranty portal
  • Confirm the model matches exactly what is on the invoice (model number, storage, colour)
  • Ensure the device boots normally and is not activation locked
  • Verify all box contents are present and original (cable, documentation)
  • For wearables/audio: check model number printed on the device against the box
